Task 6: Ray Intersect Triangle (8 points)

The purpose of this GUI is to help you test your ray intersect triangle task. Choose a ray by specifying the endpoint p0 and the direction v, and choose the three vertices of the triangle in question. Then click "compute intersection." The result will be shown in a 3D plot to the right. If there is an intersection, the intersection point will be drawn as point d, and a line segment will be extended from the ray endpoint to that point. If not, just the ray from p0 to p0 + v will be drawn.
